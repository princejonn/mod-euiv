Sometimes you want to have a really chill run, with no pressure, no stress, no worries. This is the run for you.

This mod gives you the decision to grant your nation a yearly stipend. It comes in flavours of x1, x2, x3, x5, x10, and x25.

[h1]Stipend[/h1]
[list]
	[*] +100 Ducats.
	[*] +100 Monarch Points.
	[*] +10 Legitimacy.
	[*] +10 Prestige.
	[*] +1 Yearly Income.
	[*] +1 Military Tradition.
	[*] +1 Crownland (From Estates).
	[*] +1 Loyalty (To Estates).
[/list]
